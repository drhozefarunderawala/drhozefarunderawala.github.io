About Me

Dr. Hozefa Runderawala is an accomplished Gastroenterologist, Interventional Endoscopist, and Transplant Hepatologist practicing in South Mumbai. With extensive experience and expertise, he serves as a Consultant in the Department of Gastroenterology at Saifee Hospital, Noor Hospital, Masina Hospital, Global Hospital, and Holy Family Hospital. Additionally, he holds the esteemed position of Assistant Professor of Medicine at Grant Medical College, Mumbai.

Having completed his MBBS from Grant Medical College and Sir JJ Group of Hospitals, Dr. Runderawala pursued further education, obtaining post-graduation in DNB medicine from Saifee Hospital, followed by specialized training in Gastroenterology as a DrNB from Fortis Hospital Mulund. Complementing his medical qualifications, he holds a Diploma in Hospital Administration from the prestigious TISS (Tata Institute of Social Sciences) and is Board certified in Gastroenterology and Hepatology by the Royal College of Physicians, UK (ESEGH).

Driven by a passion for research, Dr. Runderawala has contributed significantly to the medical field with multiple paper publications in national and international journals, as well as presentations at esteemed conferences worldwide.

His expertise spans across gastroenterology, hepatology, and interventional endoscopy, encompassing procedures such as Upper Gastrointestinal Endoscopy (gastroscopy), Lower Gastrointestinal Endoscopy (colonoscopy), Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography (ERCP), Percutaneous Endoscopic Gastrostomy (PEG), POEM, among others. Moreover, as a proficient liver transplant physician, he has successfully performed numerous liver transplants, facilitating the recovery of patients from various liver ailments.

Dr. Runderawala's commitment to patient care is evident through his compassionate demeanor, coupled with his unparalleled medical expertise and utilization of advanced technological treatment aids. He strives to deliver optimal medical care, ensuring the well-being and satisfaction of his patients.

Services Offered

Endoscopy

Upper Endoscopy is done using a tiny camera on the end of a long, flexible tube, to visually examine upper digestive system. A Gastroenterologist then diagnoses & treats conditions that affect the upper part of the digestive system.

Liver Transplant

Liver Transplantation or Hepatic Transplantation is the replacement of a diseased liver with the healthy liver from another person (allograft). Liver transplantation is a treatment option for end-stage liver disease and acute liver failure.

Colonoscopy

Colonoscopy is an examination of the inside of your large intestine (colon). It's helpful for diagnosing gastrointestinal diseases, such as inflammatory bowel disease and colon cancer. It can also help treat and prevent colon cancer.

ERCP

ERCP - Endoscopic Retrograde Cholangiopancreatography, is a procedure to diagnose and treat problems in the liver, gallbladder, bile ducts, and pancreas. It combines X-ray and the use of an endoscope—a long, flexible, lighted tube.
